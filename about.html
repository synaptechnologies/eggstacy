About Eggstacy

Our Story

Welcome to Eggstacy, Ghana's go-to spot for egg sandwiches & tasty fast food! We specialize in delicious egg burgers, loaded fries, and other fast food favorites that are fresh, fast, and satisfying.

Founded with a passion for creating the perfect egg sandwich, Eggstacy has quickly become a favorite among students, professionals, and food lovers in Osu and beyond. Our menu is perfect for late-night cravings and midday meals, offering a youthful, fun, and bold dining experience.

At Eggstacy, we believe that great food should be accessible to everyone. That's why we've created a menu that's not only delicious but also affordable, without compromising on quality or taste.

Our Mission

To serve the most delicious and satisfying egg-based fast food in Ghana, using fresh ingredients and innovative recipes that keep our customers coming back for more.

Our Vision

To become the most recognized fast food brand in Ghana, known for our quality, consistency, and exceptional customer experience.

Our Values

Fresh Ingredients

We source the freshest ingredients daily to ensure the highest quality in every dish we serve.

Fast Service

We understand that you're hungry, so we prioritize quick service without compromising on quality.

Customer Satisfaction

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We go above and beyond to ensure you have a great experience.
