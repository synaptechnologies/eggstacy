About Eggstacy

Welcome to Eggstacy, Ghana's go-to spot for egg sandwiches & tasty fast food! We specialize in delicious egg burgers, loaded fries, and other fast food favorites that are fresh, fast, and satisfying.

Our menu is perfect for late-night cravings and midday meals, offering a youthful, fun, and bold dining experience. Whether you're a student looking for a satisfying meal or just someone who appreciates good food, Eggstacy has something for everyone.
